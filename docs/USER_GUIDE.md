## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account

1. Navigate to **https://advanciapayledger.com/signup**
2. Enter your full name, email address, and a strong password
3. Check your inbox for a **verification email** and click the link
4. You'll be redirected to the onboarding wizard

### 2. Complete Your Profile

After verifying your email:

1. Upload a profile photo (optional)
2. Add your phone number (required for MFA)
3. Enter your date of birth and address
4. Review and accept the Terms of Service

### 3. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (Recommended)

1. Go to **Security Settings** → **Two-Factor Authentication**
2. Choose SMS or Authenticator App
3. Follow the on-screen prompts to enrol
4. Save your backup codes in a safe place

## Making Payments

### Pay for an Appointment

1. Go to **Payments** → **Make a Payment**
2. Select your **provider** from the dropdown
3. Enter the **payment amount** or select a linked invoice
4. Choose your **payment method**:
   - Credit/Debit Card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
   - Crypto Wallet (BTC, ETH, USDC)
5. Review the payment summary
6. Click **Pay Now**
7. You'll receive an email receipt and an in-app notification

### Set Up Auto-Pay

1. Go to **Settings** → **Payment Preferences**
2. Toggle **Auto-Pay** on
3. Select a default payment method
4. Choose auto-pay rules (e.g., pay invoices under $500 automatically)

### View Payment History

1. Go to **Payments** → **History**
2. Filter by date range, provider, or status
3. Click any transaction to view details
4. Download receipts as PDF

## Appointments

### Book an Appointment

1. Go to **Appointments** → **Book New**
2. Search for a provider by name, specialty, or location
3. Select an available time slot
4. Confirm the appointment
5. You'll receive a confirmation email and SMS reminder (24h before)

### Cancel or Reschedule

1. Go to **Appointments** → **Upcoming**
2. Click the appointment you want to change
3. Select **Reschedule** or **Cancel**
4. Cancellation policy: Free cancellation up to 24 hours before; late cancellations may incur a fee

## Invoices

### View Invoices

1. Go to **Invoices**
2. See all invoices with statuses: **Pending**, **Paid**, **Overdue**
3. Click an invoice to see line items
4. Pay directly from the invoice detail page

### Download Invoices

- Click the **Download PDF** button on any invoice
- Or use **Export All** to download a CSV of all invoices

---

## Disputes

If you believe a charge is incorrect:

1. Go to **Payments** → **History**
2. Find the transaction and click **Dispute**
3. Select a reason:
   - Incorrect amount
   - Service not received
   - Duplicate charge
   - Other
4. Add a description and attach any supporting documents
5. Click **Submit Dispute**
6. You'll be notified of the resolution via email

## Security Settings

### Change Password

1. Go to **Profile** → **Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password and new password
4. Click **Save**

### Manage Sessions

1. Go to **Security** → **Active Sessions**
2. Review all logged-in devices
3. Click **Revoke** to end any suspicious session

### Data Privacy (GDPR)

1. **Export Your Data**: Go to **Profile** → **Privacy** → **Export Data**
   - Generates a JSON file with all your personal data
2. **Delete Your Account**: Go to **Profile** → **Privacy** → **Delete Account**
   - This permanently removes your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
3. **Cookie Preferences**: Click the cookie icon in the footer to update your preferences

## Wallet (Crypto)

### Connect Your Wallet

1. Go to **Wallet** → **Connect**
2. Select your wallet provider (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)
3. Approve the connection in your wallet app
4. Your wallet balance will appear on the dashboard

### Make a Crypto Payment

1. During checkout, select **Crypto** as payment method
2. Choose the token (BTC, ETH, USDC)
3. Confirm in your wallet
4. Transaction confirmation typically takes 1-5 minutes

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I get a refund?**
A: Contact your provider directly or submit a dispute through the platform.

**Q: Is my health data secure?**
A: Yes. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256), HIPAA-compliant data storage, and all connections are encrypted with TLS 1.3.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, USDC).

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Email support@advanciapayledger.com or use the in-app chat (if available).
